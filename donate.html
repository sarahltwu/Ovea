Support Our Mission

Ovea is a nonprofit. Everything we offer is free, and donations are what keep it that way.

Millions of women and girls lack access to accurate health information and a safe place to ask questions. Ovea exists to close that gap, with free, expert-reviewed education and an anonymous, moderated community open to anyone.

We don't sell ads or data. Our work is funded entirely by people who believe every woman deserves to understand her own body and health. 100% of donations go to running the platform, paying our moderators and medical reviewers, and supporting our partner charities below.
