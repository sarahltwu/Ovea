About Ovea

We're a nonprofit on a mission to make trustworthy women's and girls' health education and community support free and accessible to everyone.

Our story

Ovea began with a simple, frustrating truth: too many women and girls grow up without clear answers about their own bodies, and without a safe place to ask. Health questions can feel embarrassing, information online is often unreliable, and not everyone can afford a doctor's visit for every concern.

We built Ovea to change that. We combine an anonymous, carefully moderated community with health information reviewed by gynecologists, nurses, and mental health professionals. No judgment, no ads, no selling your data, just education and support, free for everyone.

What we believe

01

Knowledge is care

Understanding your body is the first step to protecting your health. Everyone deserves clear, accurate information.

02

Safety and anonymity first

People open up when they feel safe. We protect identities and moderate every post to keep this space kind.

03

Guided by professionals

Community wisdom is powerful, but facts matter. Our medical advisors review the information we share.

04

Free, always

Health education shouldn't depend on income. Donations keep Ovea open and free for all.
